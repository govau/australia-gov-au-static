LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a business-oriented social networking service, mainly used for professional networking.  Government company pages helps LinkedIn members learn about an agency's business and job opportunities. 

Healthdirect Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Healthdirect Australia. Plays a leading role in Australia’s healthcare landscape by designing and delivering high quality telehealth, online and e-health services across the nation.

Indigenous Business Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Indigenous Business Australia. They identify and pursue opportunities that enable Indigenous Australians to create wealth, accumulate assets and achieve their financial aspirations.

Indigenous Land Corporation

Official Linkedin Company page for the Indigenous Land Corporation, a corporate Commonwealth entity established in 1995 to assist Indigenous people to acquire and manage land to achieve economic, environmental, social and cultural benefits.

IP Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for IP Australia. IP Australia celebrates and supports the bright ideas and inventive spirit of Australians. As the Government agency responsible for administering IP rights in Australia, they receive and process patent, trade mark, designs and plant breeder's rights applications, conduct hearings and decide on disputed matters relating to granting or refusing Australian IP rights.

LiveCorp - Australian Livestock Export Corporation

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australian Livestock Export Corporation. LiveCorp is a not-for-profit industry body established to undertake livestock export industry services to promote Australian livestock overseas and undertake industry research for the benefit of Australian livestock exporters.

MindMatters, Australia

Official Linkedin Showcase page for MindMatters, a mental health initiative for secondary schools that aims to improve the mental health and wellbeing of young people.

myfuture.edu.au

Official Linkedin Showcase page for www.myfuture.edu.au, Australia’s free online career exploration and information service that assists career planning, career pathways and work transitions.

National Centre for Vocational Education Research (NCVER)

Official Linkedin Company page for the National Centre for Vocational Education Research. NCVER collects and analyses data about vocational students and the courses they study, apprenticeships and traineeships, and survey students on the outcomes of their training and employers about how they use training to meet their skill needs.

National Disability Insurance Agency

Official Linkedin Company page for the National Disability Insurance Agency. It is an independent statutory agency whose role is to implement the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS), which will support a better life for hundreds of thousands of Australians with a significant and permanent disability and their families and carers.

National Innovation and Science Agenda

Official Linkedin Showcase page for National Innovation and Science Agenda which will drive smart ideas that create business growth, local jobs and global success.

National Portrait Gallery, Canberra

Official Linkedin Company page for the National Portrait Gallery, Canberra. We cultivate understanding and appreciation of the Australian people – our identity, history, culture, creativity and diversity – through portraiture.

nbn Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for nbn Australia. The nbn™ network is delivering the technology and infrastructure for all Australians to realise their dreams and discover new possibilities in a connected world.

New Colombo Plan Alumni Program

Official Linkedin Showcase page for the New Colombo Plan Alumni Program. It supports alumni of the Australian Government's New Colombo Plan to maintain a connection to the program, each other and the Indo-Pacific.

Office of the Children's eSafety Commissioner

Official Linkedin Company page for the Office of the Children's eSafety Commissioner. The Office aims to help guide young people toward safe, enjoyable experiences online, by providing educational resources and advice to parents, educators and young people.

Plant Biosecurity Cooperative Research Centre

Official Linkedin Company page for the Plant Biosecurity Cooperative Research Centre. They have an extensive collaborative network of researchers and educators from 27 participating Australian and international organisations, representing industry, research organisations, universities, and Australian state and federal governments.

Reserve Bank of Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Reserve Bank of Australia, Australia's central bank. Its duty is to contribute to the maintenance of price stability, full employment, and the economic prosperity and welfare of the Australian people.

Safe Work Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Safe Work Australia. SWA leads the development of national policy to improve work health and safety and workers’ compensation arrangements across Australia.

Screen Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Screen Australia. Screen Australia is the key Federal Government agency for the Australian film and TV industry. Our role is to support and promote the development of a highly creative, innovative and commercially sustainable Australian screen production industry.

Sugar Research Australia Limited

Official Linkedin Company page for Sugar Research Australia Limited. SRA invests in and manages a portfolio of research, development and extension (RD&E) projects that drive productivity, profitability and sustainability for the Australian sugarcane industry. It focuses on national RD&E priorities and delivering good science at a local level.

Tax Practitioners Board

Official Linkedin Company page for the Tax Practitioners Board. The TPB is a national body responsible for the registration and regulation of tax agents, BAS agents and tax (financial) advisers (collectively referred to as 'tax practitioners'). The TPB is also responsible for ensuring compliance with the Tax Agent Services Act 2009), including the Code of Professional Conduct.

Tourism Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Tourism Australia, the Australian Government agency responsible for attracting international visitors to Australia, both for leisure and business events.

Trade and Investment

Official Linkedin Showcase page for Trade and Investment from the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade. Stay up to date with the latest from DFAT's trade and investment specialists, including: trade negotiations, FTAs, investment and business insights from DFAT's global network.

Wine Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Wine Australia. Wine Australia supports a competitive wine sector by investing in research, development and extension (RD&E), growing domestic and international markets and protecting the reputation of Australian wine.
