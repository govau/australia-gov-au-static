Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency

Parent Portfolio: Education and Training

The Tertiary Education Quality Standards Agency (TEQSA) is an independent statutory authority. TEQSA regulates and assures the quality of Australia’s large, diverse and complex higher education sector.
