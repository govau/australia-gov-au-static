Department of Industry, Innovation and Science - Resources


Key web pages and entry points for the Department of Industry, Innovation and Science

Business - Starting a business

business.gov.au provides a how-to guide and a list of issues to consider when you're thinking of starting a business.

Business grants and assistance

Locate the grants and assistance programmes most relevant to your business. Grants are freely available from the Australian, state and territory governments. You can search through a wide variety of grants in many categories.

Employ people

Recruiting and keeping the right people can play a big role in achieving business success. Provides links to helpful information on a range of topics relating to taking on an employee and creating a healthy workplace.

Events and training for business

Find government-funded events, seminars, training courses and workshops for business held around Australia.

Fair trading

Australian federal and state laws protect business and customers from unfair trading practices. Provides information about things to consider when ensuring your business meets fair trading laws.

Finding government statistics

Provides links to a range of business-related government statistics and resources both nation-wide and for your state or territory.

For Employers – apprenticeships

Information for employers on all aspects of Australian Apprenticeships, including costs and benefits for your business and where to get more information.

Heritage grants

Use the grant finder to search for heritage related grants that are available from both Australian and state and territory government.

Home-based business

Provides information for setting up and running a home-based business including: advice and support; planning; registration and licences for home-based businesses; taxation and insurance.

IP – Protect your ideas

Ideas and unique creations can exist in every business. Intellectual property (IP) laws allow for protection of these through patents, trade marks, copyright, designs, circuit layouts, and plant breeder's rights.

Import and export

Provides information about customs requirements, government regulations, quarantine controls, and financial assistance for businesses wanting to import or export goods.

Industry – budget

Links to the Department's portfolio budget statements.

Industry, Innovation and Science annual report

Annual reports of the Department of Industry, Innovation and Science and of the former Department of Industry, Department of Industry, Innovation, Climate Change, Science, Research and Tertiary Education, and Department of Resources, Energy and Tourism.

Information and communications grants

Use the grant finder to search for information media and telecommunications related grants that are available from both Australian and state and territory government.

Insurance and workers' compensation

Provides information about the different types of insurance that may be required for running your business and compulsory insurances such as workers' compensation.

Online business

Find information about getting connected, securing your website, IT training, buying and selling online, and legal and consumer obligations.

Pay and conditions

Wages and employment conditions are often one of the first things a potential employee will consider about a job. Provides links to a range of information on employee entitlements and pay.

Primary industry grants

Use the grant finder to search for primary industry related grants that are available from both Australian and state and territory government.

Prime Minister's Prizes for Science

The nation's finest awards for excellence in science and science teaching.

Resources

Provides information on Australian Government policies and programs relating to resources. Covers upstream petroleum, mining, enhancing Australia's resources sector, and radioactive waste management.

Science – science.gov.au

Find out more about the Australian Government’s commitment to and investment in science.

Skills and training

Assistance and support to help businesses get the skills they need, to be flexible and competitive in the global market.

Standards and conformance infrastructure-Australia

Australia has a world class standards and conformance infrastructure which underpins and supports virtually every aspect of our everyday life. A number of bodies comprise this infrastructure.

More

  • National Measurement Institute
  • Standards Australia Limited
  • National Association of Testing Authorities
  • Joint Accreditation System of Australia and New Zealand

Tax

Understanding taxes and meeting taxation obligations is essential for any business. Understand the different types of taxations that may apply to your business, and how to register.

Tenders and contracts

Selling goods and services to government is an important opportunity for all businesses. Provides information about finding tender opportunities in Australia.

Training grants

Use the grant finder to search for training related grants that are available from both Australian and state and territory government.

White Paper on Developing Northern Australia

Provides information on the development of the White Paper and sets out a policy platform for realising the full economic potential of the north.

Workplace health and safety

Business owners have responsibilities regarding health and safety in the workplace. This page links to information on WHS/OH&S Acts, regulations and codes of practice and workers' compensation requirements.

More

  • WHS/OH&S acts, regulations and codes of practice in your state or territory

business.gov.au checklists

Are you new to business? The 'Starting your business checklist' covers many of the basic issues you need to consider. The checklists will be updated on a regular basis; please check that you have the latest version.
