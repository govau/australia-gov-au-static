Australia New Zealand Policing Advisory Agency

The Australia New Zealand Policing Advisory Agency (ANZPAA) provides policing policy and strategic advice, research capacity, knowledge management and information sharing services to Police Ministers and Commissioners throughout the Australian Commonwealth, its States and Territories, and New Zealand. ANZPAA is not an operational policing agency.
