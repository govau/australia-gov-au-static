A-Z of Government Services

National Relay Service

An Australia-wide telephone access service provided for people who are deaf or have a hearing or speech impairment. It is also available to anyone who wants to call a person with a hearing or speech impairment.

Department of Communications and the Arts

Norfolk Island Birds

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

From iconic species like the green parrot and the boobook owl, the island is home to a mixture of land, water and seabirds, some of which are found nowhere else in the world. Discover some of this birdlife by exploring the images, bird calls and detailed descriptions of nearly 30 birds. Included is information on walks and itineraries.

Director of National Parks

Norfolk Island National Park

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Norfolk Island is a place of great natural beauty, and historical significance. This app offers maps, places to eat, accommodation options and background information on the most commonly sighted wildlife,

Director of National Parks

Nurse-On-Call [Vic]

For immediate, expert health advice from a registered nurse, 24 hours a day, call 1300 606 024, for the cost of a local call from anywhere in Victoria.  Calls from mobile phones may be charged at a higher rate.

Department of Health [Vic]

NZAUConnect

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Simultaneously search both the New Zealand Companies Office (NZCO) and the Australian Securities and Investment Commission (ASIC) company and business names registers.

Australian Securities and Investments Commission

On Track with The Right Mix

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Track the number and types of drinks you consume; the amount of money it's costing you and calculate the impact the alcohol has on your well-being and fitness levels.

Department of Veterans' Affairs

Online estimators (DHS)

Choose an estimator to calculate your entitlements e.g Centrelink payments, child support and child care. Online estimators let you estimate or compare payments, based on your current or proposed circumstances.

Department of Human Services

Open government [ACT]

Access to information held by the ACT government that can be made publicly available.

Chief Minister and Treasury Directorate [ACT]

Operation Life

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Supports clinical care by offering easy access to emergency and professional support services and a personal support network that users can call when help is needed. Designed for use by serving and ex-serving Australian Defence Force (ADF) personnel who are at risk of suicide, as a support to clinical care.

Department of Veterans' Affairs

Organ donor registration

The only national register for people to record their decision about becoming an organ and tissue donor for transplantation after death. Family consent is always sought before donation can proceed.

Department of Human Services

OzHealth

Download for:
  • iOS

Provides access to facts and figures on the health and wellbeing of Australians. Topics covered include: diseases and conditions, risk factors, health spending, alcohol and tobacco and an overview of the health of population groups.

Australian Institute of Health and Welfare

OzWelfare

Download for:
  • iOS

Provides statistics and fast facts on: welfare spending, the wellbeing of population groups including children, young people, working age and older people, as well as information on diversity and disadvantage in Australia.

Australian Institute of Health and Welfare

Passport information service (APIS)

For passport information call 131 232 from inside Australia seven days a week, for the cost of a local call.  APIS is not a passport office.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Passport renewal

To renew your Australian passport using the renewal form, you will need a passport that was issued in your current name, be valid for at least two years when issued, be an Australian citizen and aged 18 years or over.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Pay your fines

Provides links to state and territory online payment facilities for traffic and other fines.

Digital Transformation Agency

Pelvic Floor First

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Customised workouts allow people of all fitness levels and pelvic floor function to undertake pelvic floor safe exercises to reduce the risk of bladder and bowel problems.

Continence Foundation of Australia

Pelvic Floor First

Encourages pelvic floor safe exercises so that people don't experience unwanted side effects such as bladder and bowel problems.

Continence Foundation of Australia

People with disability

Services and payments are available to help people who have an illness, injury or disability.

Department of Human Services

Permanent migrants or temporary visitors tax file number application

If you have a visa with work rights, a permanent migration visa, an overseas student visa, or a valid visa to stay in Australia indefinitely you can apply for a tax file number (TFN) online.

Australian Taxation Office

Permits licences and leases for activities in Commonwealth Parks and Reserves

If you wish to conduct commercial activities in a Commonwealth Park or Reserve – such as events, tours, commercial filming and photography – you need a permit. This page provides full details and downloads for permits.

Department of the Environment and Energy

Police checks (criminal history records checks)

A police check may be required for people seeking employment in Australia or overseas, visa applications and other purposes. Provides links to national and state and territory police records check services.

Digital Transformation Agency

Police services – states and territories

State and territory police services have responsibility for community safety and protection within their jurisdiction. Provides links to state and territory police services.

Digital Transformation Agency

Portrait Stories – all about the art and the people in the National Portrait Gallery

Download for:
  • iOS

A digital companion to the National Portrait Gallery collection. Use it to peruse the portraits and learn more about the people who have shaped Australia.

National Portrait Gallery

Postage calculator

Postal charges vary depending on the item and its destination. Get an estimated price for sending letters and parcels to local and international destinations.

Australian Postal Corporation

Pregnancy Pelvic Floor Plan

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Teaches you how to exercise your pelvic floor muscles to maintain or improve bladder control and also provides practical information and tips on bladder and bowel health.

Continence Foundation of Australia
