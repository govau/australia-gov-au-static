A-Z of Government Services

State parliaments and members' contacts

Australia has a federal system of government with a national parliament and legislative assemblies and councils (parliaments) in each state and territory. Find links to state parliaments and members contact information.

Digital Transformation Agency

States, Territories and Local Government

Entry points, government directories, listings of departments and agencies and parliamentary contacts on state and territory websites

Digital Transformation Agency

SunSmart

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Lets you find a 7 day weather forecast and the UV level and sun protection times for your local area in Australia. Personalise the sun protection alert to remind you of the daily sun protection times or use the vitamin D Tracker to find out if you are getting enough UV exposure. Sun protection times are based on the UV information issued daily by the Bureau of Meteorology.

SunSmart Victoria

Super information for temporary residents departing Australia

If you work while visiting Australia on a temporary resident visa, your employer may be required to pay superannuation (super) on your behalf to a super fund. Explains what you can do with your super after you leave Australia.

Australian Taxation Office

Take a Stand Together

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Provides students with tips and advice which they can apply to different bullying situations.

Safe and Supportive School Communities Project

Tax Help

If you are on a low income and want some free and confidential help filling out your tax return the Australian Taxation Office trained Tax Help volunteers may be able to assist.

Australian Taxation Office

Tax – update your details

You can update your address, nominated representative and contact details held by the ATO using the online services. You can update your name, gender or date of birth only by phone, mail or in person as you may require supporting documents.

Australian Taxation Office

The Allen Adventure

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS iPad

This fun and interactive app teaches children social and emotional skills and how to identify and deal with inappropriate behaviour from other children. Suitable for children from 3 to 8 years of age.

Safe and Supportive School Communities Project

ThinkUKnow

A free, evidence-based cyber safety program providing cyber safety education to parents, carers and teachers through schools and organisations across Australia.

ThinkUKnow Australia

Time zones and daylight saving

Provides information about time zones and daylight saving time across Australia.

Digital Transformation Agency

Tourist Refund Scheme

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS
  • Windows Phone
  • Web

Allows you to enter the information required to lodge a TRS claim. The information is stored in a QR code which can be processed at a number of international airports.

Department of Home Affairs

Track your item

Many items sent through the Australia Post delivery network have tracking capabilities, allowing you to view where your item is during the delivery process.

Australian Postal Corporation

TrackMyGOALS

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

The app allows you to set, plan, track and manage your savings goals, helping you to cultivate good savings habits goals.

Australian Securities and Investments Commission

TrackMySPEND

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Track your personal expenses on the go with this easy-to-use app from MoneySmart. TrackMySPEND gives you a clear picture of what you are spending your money on.

Australian Securities and Investments Commission

Translating and Interpreting Service

An interpreting service for people who do not speak English and for agencies and businesses that need to communicate with their non-English speaking clients. The majority of TIS National services are free to non-English speakers. Generally the organisation you are contacting will accept the charges for the service.

Department of Home Affairs

Travel advisories – Smartraveller

Subscribe to Smartraveller travel advisories for the destinations you intend to visit and receive alerts, via email, each time the travel advice for one of your destinations is updated.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

TravelSafe Australia

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Multilingual app designed for tourists and travellers to Australia. It provides information about disasters and advice on what to do, how to find nearby resources and links to further safety information.

Australian Institute for Disaster Resilience

Triple Zero Kids' Challenge

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Kids will learn how to deal confidently with getting help in an emergency, by playing games and solving problems. They'll learn about safety messages and hear what happens when you call Triple Zero (000).

Triple Zero Awareness Working Group

Truck Buyers Guide

Choosing a small or medium sized truck or commercial vehicle can be a complicated exercise. The guide provides a step by step guide to help you make a well informed choice that suits your business needs while minimising fuel consumption.

Department of Infrastructure, Regional Development and Cities

Uluru Birds

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Discover some of Uluru-Kata Tjuta's birdlife by exploring the images, bird calls and detailed descriptions of over 50 birds. Included is information on walks and itineraries.

Director of National Parks

Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Uncover the best that Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park has to offer. From Audio tours and trip planning to maps and contact numbers, you'll have all the information you need.

Director of National Parks

Visa Entitlement Verification Online (VEVO)

VEVO is a secure online service which allows you to check and email your visa details, and provides information about your visa conditions. With your permission, VEVO can also give registered organisations a limited amount of relevant information on your visa conditions, for example if you are permitted to work or study in Australia.

Department of Home Affairs

Visa Finder

Helps you find the Australian visa most likely to meet your specific circumstances.

Department of Home Affairs

Visit Booderee National Park

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Discover the best of Booderee National Park, located at Jervis Bay on the South Coast of New South Wales. Included is information on activities, camping, maps, cultural heritage and itineraries.

Director of National Parks

Visitor visa (subclass 600)

Provides a visa for people travelling to Australia as a tourist, to see family or for business for up to three, six or 12 months.
