Caring for children

Find government information and services to assist parents and carers responsible for the wellbeing of children.

Raising Children Network

Download for:
  • Raising Children Network

Provides reliable information that can help parents with the day-to-day work of raising children and looking after their own needs. Covers a broad range of up-to-date parenting topics.

Australian Police Child ID

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS
Helps parents and guardians easily collect and send important information about their child/children to police in an event of a disappearance or abduction. No data from this app is collected or stored by the police unless it is sent to them.
