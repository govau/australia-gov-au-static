A-Z of Government Services

Booderee Birds

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Booderee National Park is rich in bird life with over 200 species recorded. Discover some of its birdlife by exploring the images, bird calls and detailed descriptions of nearly 50 birds. Included is information on walks and itineraries.

Director of National Parks

Border Watch

Department's programme that receives information about suspicious activities. You can call Border Watch, on 1800 009 623.

Department of Home Affairs

Breast implant information

Provides information regarding poly implant protheses (PIP) breast implants. The 24 hours information line 1800 217 257 can assist individuals concerned about breast implants.

Department of Health

Breast prostheses reimbursement program

Provides reimbursement of up to $400 for new or replacement external breast prostheses for eligible Australian women who have had a mastectomy as a result of breast cancer.

Department of Human Services

Breastfeeding helpline

Available 7 days a week on 1800 686 268, it is staffed by trained, volunteer counsellors who answer calls on a roster system.

Australian Breastfeeding Association

Building approval [ACT]

Building approval is required for most developments in the Australian Capital Territory to ensure building complies with building laws. Includes links to exemptions information and building approval information pack.

Environment and Sustainable Development Directorate

Business name registration

Find out about registering a business name in Australia. A National Business Names Registration System managed by ASIC has replaced the state and territory registers.

Australian Securities and Investments Commission

Can I pack that? – Dangerous Goods App

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Are the items you’re packing safe to fly? Helps inform you about what you can and can’t pack in your luggage. And, if it can be brought aboard, guide you on how to do it safely.

Civil Aviation Safety Authority

Canberra Guide

Download for:
  • Android
  • iOS

Visitors can plan their trip to the Nation's Capital and search for events, attractions and public facilities in the territory. .

National Capital Authority

Cancer Screening

Provides supporting information for campaigns to protect your health through early detection, even if you don’t have any symptoms of the disease. Simple screening tests look for particular changes and early signs of cancer.

More

  • BreastScreen Australia
  • National Bowel Cancer Screening Program
  • National Cervical Screening Program

 

Department of Health

Carers

Caring daily for someone with a disability, illness or medical condition is an important responsibility. Payments and services are available to help.

More

  • Payments for carers

Department of Human Services

Centrelink Confirmation eServices for businesses

Provides online access to real time information about Centrelink customers so you can confirm a customer's eligibility for your rebates, concessions or services.

Department of Human Services

Centrelink Indigenous Phone Services

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people can get access to Centrelink services from anywhere in Australia, by calling Freecall 1800 136 380 Monday to Friday 8.00 am to 5.00 pm (local times).

Department of Human Services

Cervical screening registers

Cervical screening registers operate in each state and territory and keep a confidential record of your test results. To advise of a change of address, or if you would prefer to have your details removed from a register, provides links to your state or territory cervical screening register.

Department of Health

Change my address

If you're moving house there's a range of people and organisations that you need to notify.

Digital Transformation Agency

Change my address (WA)

If you have changed your address you will need to complete a form in writing or submit your new address details via the change of address web form.

Department of Transport [WA]

Change of address (Qld)

You can notify the Queensland Government about changes to your home and/or postal address online, rather than contacting multiple Queensland Government departments/services.

Queensland Government

Change of address (Tas)

An online form to notify a range of Tasmanian Government services that you have changed your address, including: driver licence, vehicle registration, firearms services, seniors card, breast screen register, Tasmanian cervical screening register and many more.

Tasmanian Government

Change of address - VicRoads

Update your Victorian driver licence or registration details online today.

VicRoads

Change of address details (SA)

Use the EzyReg online facility to change your address details or vehicle registration address details in South Australia.

Department of Planning, Transport and Infrastructure [SA]

Change of address – RMS (NSW)

Change your address details with the Roads and Maritime Services (New South Wales) online, by phone, or by visiting a motor registry.

Roads and Maritime Services [NSW]

Change your address - Motor Vehicle Registry (NT)

Change your address details with the Northern Territory Motor Vehicle Registry. Changes can be made online, by phone or by mail.

Department of Transport [NT]

Changed your name? Make sure your passport is in your current name.

You may wish to change the name that appears in your passport, due to marriage, divorce etc. In most instances, you will need to provide evidence of your name change from an Australian Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Child alert requests – passports

A Child Alert is a warning to the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade that there may be circumstances to be considered before an Australian passport is issued to a child.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Child Care Access Hotline

The hotline on 1800 670 305 provides information about the types of child care available and vacancies. It operates 8am to 6pm nationally Monday to Friday, and is a free call unless calling from a mobile.

Department of Education and Training
