Help and contact

Contacting australia.gov.au

Please do not contact australia.gov.au for information on:

  • the progress of your visa application,
  • your eligibility for benefits or assistance, or
  • what extra information there is applicable to your personal circumstances.

We don't have access to the information required to answer questions. Therefore we are unable to respond. To answer these types of questions you will need to contact the relevant agency directly.
