Parliamentary publications

The following is a list of key resources available from the Parliament of Australia. In addition, each parliamentary department produces its own publications which are accessible from the Parliament of Australia website.

  • ParlInfo search
  • Bills
  • Hansard
  • Committee reports
  • Parliamentary papers
  • Parliamentary Handbook

ParlInfo search

ParlInfo search

ParlInfo search lets you search and obtain Australian Parliamentary information including hansards, bills, senate journals, votes and proceedings, notice papers, committee reports, the Parliamentary Handbook, newspaper clippings, media, publications and much more.

Bills

A bill is a proposal for a law or a change to an existing law. A bill becomes law (an Act) when agreed to in identical form by both houses of Parliament and assented to by the Governor-General.

Bills and legislation

Includes links to bills being considered by Parliament and awaiting Royal Assent and previous bills that have received Royal Assent and bills otherwise disposed of.

ParlInfo Search

Bills information can also be searched via ParlInfo Search. Enter your search terms and refine your results by selecting dates. You can also choose to search Second Reading Speeches for a Bill.

Commonwealth bills and explanatory memoranda sales

Arrangements applying for purchases or orders of Commonwealth bills and explanatory memoranda.

Hansard

Hansard

Hansard is the name given to the edited transcripts of debates in the Senate, House of Representatives, Main Committee and parliamentary committees. They are published shortly after the chamber or committee proceedings have concluded. The electronic version of Hansard is available from 1981 onwards.

ParlInfo Search

Hansard can also be searched via ParlInfo Search. Enter your search term/s; tick Hansard boxes under both House of Representatives and Senate. Narrow your search further by using the Search Parameters to the right of the Hansard boxes.

Go to top

Committee reports

Parliamentary committees conduct inquiries into specified matters which includes taking submissions, hearing witnesses, sifting evidence, discussing matters in detail and formulating reasoned conclusions.

Senate and other Senate-based committees

Links to current inquiries, hearings, submissions and transcripts of Senate committees.

Register of Senate committee reports (1970-2013)

Register comprises Chronological listing of reports; Chronological listing by committee; Chronological listing by subject matter and year of tabling.

Significant reports

Significant reports tabled before 1997 listed by committee with the date tabled.

House of Representatives and other House-based committees

A complete list of inquiries, reports, submissions and transcripts.

Register of Committee reports

A complete list of all House of Representative and Joint Committee Reports from 1901 to the current day. The list of reports can be browsed by Parliament, Committee Name or Subject.
