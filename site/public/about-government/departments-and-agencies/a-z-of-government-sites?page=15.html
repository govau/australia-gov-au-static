A–Z of government sites

Cotton Research and Development Corporation

Works with industry to invest in research, development and extension activities for a more profitable, sustainable and dynamic cotton industry.

Cotton Research and Development Corporation

Council of Australasian Tribunals

Facilitates liaison and discussion between tribunals to examine and compare ideas, working methods, organisation and management, member trainings and support programs.

Administrative Appeals Tribunal

Council of Australian Governments

As the peak intergovernmental forum in Australia, COAG initiates, develops and monitors the implementation of policy reforms that are of national significance and which require cooperative action by Australian governments.

Council of Australian Governments (COAG)

Council of Financial Regulators

Coordinating body for Australia's main financial regulatory agencies. Contributes to the efficiency and effectiveness of financial regulation and promotes stability of the Australian financial system.

Reserve Bank of Australia

Council of Heads of Australasian Herbaria

Comprises those administratively responsible for the major Australian Herbaria. Promotes all matters of interest to herbaria in Australia and increases cooperation and understanding between herbaria.

Council of Heads of Australasian Herbaria

Council on Federal Financial Relations

Oversees the operation of the Intergovernmental Agreement on Federal Financial Relations which provides the overarching framework for the Commonwealth's financial relations with the States.

Council on Federal Financial Relations

Country of Origin Labelling

Information for businesses, consumers and trading partners about the new food labels that make it clear where the products you buy are produced, grown, made or packed.

Department of Industry, Innovation and Science

Creative Partnerships Australia

Promotes private sector support for the arts and builds the capacity of the arts, business and donors to connect.

Creative Partnerships Australia

Criminology Research Grants Program

Undertakes and provides funding for criminological research which is relevant to public policy and promotes the value and use of such research.

Australian Institute of Criminology

CSIRO blog

Be entertained, informed and inspired by a blog on all the latest and greatest science from Australia’s national science organisation, the CSIRO.

CSIRO, the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

CSIRO GrazPlan

Decision support (DS) tools intended to help farmers and farm advisors make decisions about farm management. Also useful for industry organisations, agribusiness, banks and insurance companies.

CSIRO, the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

CSIRO Publishing

An independent science and technology publisher covering a wide range of scientific disciplines. Browse their online catalogue of books, magazines and peer-reviewed journals.

CSIRO, the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

CSIRO – Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

One of the world's largest and most diverse scientific and industrial research organisations, supporting Australia's industry needs and delivering education programs for schools, teachers and the wider community.

CSIRO, the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

Cutting Red Tape

An online resource for regulation reform, it includes the Australian Government’s Guide to Regulation. written to to help make better regulation, not more regulation.

Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet

Dairy Australia

Works with dairy farmers and the industry to invest in research, development and extension projects to help farmers adapt to changing operating environments, and achieve a profitable, sustainable dairy industry.

Dairy Australia

Data Exchange

Collects data from service providers of client-based programmes in the Department of Social Services as well as some programmes from the Department of Health and Attorney-General’s Department. Also shares this information back with them through self-service reports.

Department of Social Services

data.gov.au

Provides an easy way to find, access and reuse public data from the Australian Government and state and territory governments. We encourage you to use government data to analyse, mashup and develop tools and applications to benefit all Australians.

More

  • Open Data Toolkit

Digital Transformation Agency

Data.gov.au blog

Discussions on open government data and datasets.

Digital Transformation Agency

Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples

Affirms the minimum standards for the survival, dignity, security and well-being of Indigenous peoples worldwide and enshrines Indigenous peoples’ right to be different. 

Australian Human Rights Commission

DEFENCE 2020

An educational program to help students investigate the relevance of Defence issues to their own lives. A middle secondary curriculum resource for SOSE, English and Civics and Citizenship.

Department of Defence

Defence Abuse Response Taskforce

The Taskforce was established to assess and respond to individual cases of sexual and other abuse in Defence occurring before 11 April 2011.

Attorney-General's Department

Defence Community Organisation

DCO provides a broad range of services in support of members and their families to help them balance the demands of military service with personal and family commitments.

Department of Defence

Defence Force Discipline Appeal Tribunal

Hears and determines appeals from courts martial and Defence Force magistrates in respect of service offences by Australian Defence Force personnel.

Defence Force Discipline Appeal Tribunal

Defence Force Remuneration Tribunal

Determines pay and pay related allowances for the regular and reserve members of the Australian Defence Force (ADF).

Defence Force Remuneration Tribunal

Defence Force Retirement and Death Benefits Scheme

DFRDB is a fully defined untaxed benefit superannuation scheme. It was closed to new members in 1991 and was replaced by MilitarySuper.

Commonwealth Superannuation Corporation
