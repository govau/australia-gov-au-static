    A–Z of government sites

    Hearing Help

    Get impartial hearing advice and support from fully qualified hearing specialists on freecall 1800 740 301 or via webchat. Hearing Help includes information and tips and a free hearing test.

    Australian Hearing

    Hearing Services Program

    Provides access to information on the hearing services program for eligible clients and includes details for hearing professionals, FAQ's, factsheets, forms and publications.

    Department of Health

    HEIMSHelp

    Provides information about requirements and procedures for higher education and VET providers.

    Department of Education and Training

    High Court Digital Collection

    Provides a searchable collection of High Court judgements from 1948 to the present and scanned copies of the judgments comprised in the first 100 Commonwealth Law Reports.

    High Court of Australia

    High Court of Australia

    Hears matters involving a dispute about the meaning of the Constitution, as well as final appeals in civil and criminal matters from all courts in Australia.

    High Court of Australia

    HMB Endeavour Virtual Tour

    A free, curriculum-led, virtual tour of the HMB Endeavour. Using 360 images and video, your virtual host will guide your students through the historical context of James Cook’s scientific voyage.

    Australian National Maritime Museum

    Hobby or business - help me work it out

    This tool helps artists, creatives and makers to work out if they are more like a business or a hobby and what government obligations they have.

    Department of Industry, Innovation and Science

    Horticulture Innovation Australia

    Invests in research and development and marketing for the best solutions and outcomes for growers and horticulture.

    Horticulture Innovation Australia

    Human Research Ethics Portal

    The National Approach enables the recognition of a single ethical and scientific review of multi-centre human research within and/or across Australian jurisdictions.

    More

    • National Ethics Application Form

    National Health and Medical Research Council

    Human Rights Awards

    Provides the shortlists and the winners for the Human Rights Medal, Young People's Human Rights Medal, Law Award, Business Award, Racism. It Stops With Me Award, the Tony Fitzgerald Memorial Community Award and the Media Award. 

    Australian Human Rights Commission

    Immunise Australia Program

    Provides supporting information for a campaign to increase national immunisation rates by funding free vaccination programs. Provides information about immunisation to the general public and health professionals.

    Department of Health

    In Work and In Play: Building the Canberra Community

    Celebrating the role the Australian Public Service (APS) has played in shaping Canberra over the past 100 years. The project explores four themes relating to life in the APS and Canberra: community, at home, workplace and defining moments.

    National Capital Authority

    Independent Directory

    Matches skilled directors to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander corporations.

    Office of the Registrar of Indigenous Corporations

    Independent Expert Scientific Committee on Coal Seam Gas and Large Coal Mining Development

    Provides scientific advice to decision makers on the impact that coal seam gas and large coal mining development may have on Australia's water resources.

    Department of the Environment and Energy

    Independent Hospital Pricing Authority

    Contributes to significant reforms to improve Australian public hospitals. A major component of these reforms is the implementation of national Activity Based Funding (ABF) for Australian public hospitals.

    Independent Hospital Pricing Authority

    Indigenous Advancement Support

    Employers looking to support and employ cadets will now be able to apply for cadetship funding through the Tailored Assistance Employment Grant (TAEG) local PM&C Regional Network offices.

    Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet

    Indigenous Business Australia

    Assists Indigenous Australians to buy their own homes, own their own businesses and invest in commercial ventures that provide strong financial returns.

    Indigenous Business Australia

    Indigenous Careers

    Brings together useful tips about Australian Government jobs opportunities, applications, interviews and career profiles for Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander people.

    Australian Public Service Commission

    Indigenous Jobs and Training Review

    Review tasked to consider ways that training and employment services can better link to the commitment of employers to provide further sustainable employment opportunities for Indigenous people.

    Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet

    Indigenous Justice Clearinghouse

    Promotes discussion and disseminates relevant Indigenous justice information to government policy makers and those working in the Indigenous justice field.

    Australian Institute of Criminology

    Indigenous Land Corporation

    Assists Indigenous Australians to acquire land and to manage Indigenous held land so as to provide economic, environmental, social or cultural benefits.

    Indigenous Land Corporation

    indigenous.gov.au

    Connects Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people with Australian Government policies and programmes and raises awareness about the initiatives that affect them most.

    Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet

    Infrastructure and Project Financing Agency

    Advising the Australian Government on funding and financing solutions.

    Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet

    Infrastructure Australia

    Prioritises and progresses nationally significant infrastructure. Provides research and advice to governments and the community on the projects and reforms Australia needs to fill the infrastructure gap.

    Department of Infrastructure, Regional Development and Cities

    Infrastructure Investment

    Provides information on national infrastructure investment programmes providing targeted funding for land transport projects.

    Department of Infrastructure, Regional Development and Cities
