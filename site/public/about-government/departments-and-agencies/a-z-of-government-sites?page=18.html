    A–Z of government sites

    Education at the National Museum of Australia

    Curriculum linked classroom resources and school programs for teachers and students. Includes case studies, free units of work, online games and videos across Australian history, Indigenous culture and history, the environment and more.

    National Museum of Australia

    Education Council

    Provides a forum for collaboration between jurisdictions with responsibility for education.

    COAG Education Council

    Education for Young People

    For teachers and students, this toolkit of disaster resilience education resources focuses on both individual and community resilience.

    Australian Institute for Disaster Resilience

    Education Services Australia

    Provides education services to support delivery of national priorities and initiatives in the schools, training and higher education sectors.

    eex.gov.au – Energy Efficiency Exchange

    Intended for use by medium to large Australian energy-using companies, eex.gov.au supports the development and implementation of energy management and energy efficiency strategies.

    Department of the Environment and Energy

    Election Costings 2016

    During the caretaker period for a general election the Treasury and the Department of Finance may be requested to cost election commitments.

    Department of Finance

    Election Returns

    Candidates, unendorsed Senate groups and Senate groups endorsed by more than one registered political party must disclose donations and electoral expenditure incurred. Election disclosure returns are made available for public inspection 24 weeks after polling day.

    Australian Electoral Commission

    Election Speeches

    Collection of speeches by successful and unsuccessful Prime Ministerial candidates from every election from 1901 up to the present day. More than just historical records; they tell us about national concerns and political obsessions.

    Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

    Electoral Council of Australia and New Zealand

    A consultative council of Electoral Commissioners and Chief Electoral Officers from the electoral authorities of the Commonwealth, states and territories and New Zealand.

    Electoral Council of Australia & New Zealand

    Electrical Regulatory Authorities Council

    Works towards the coordination between the technical and safety electrical regulatory authorities of the Australian states, territories and Commonwealth. and New Zealand.

    Electrical Regulatory Authorities Council

    Emergency Alert

    Issues warnings to landline and mobile telephones linked to the addresses (properties and houses) within a geographical area affected by an emergency. Warnings will also be sent to mobile telephones based on the last known location of the handset at the time of an emergency.

    Emergency Alert Australia

    Emissions Calculator

    Developed for Marine Park tourism operators to allow easy calculation of their carbon footprint.

    Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority

    Employer Administration Centre

    Provides information for employers about the CSS, PSS, PSSap, MilitarySuper and DFRDB schemes.

    Commonwealth Superannuation Corporation

    Energy Made Easy

    Helps you compare electricity and gas offers for residential and small business and allows you to compare your residential electricity usage against other similar households.

    Australian Energy Regulator

    Energy Rating

    Find the most efficient product for you. The Energy Rating label enables consumers to compare the energy efficiency of domestic appliances. Choose energy efficient appliances and products in all sectors (residential, commercial and industrial).

    Department of the Environment and Energy

    engage.dss.gov.au

    Helps you find and participate in Department of Social Services consultations. Share your views and help shape their policies.

    Department of Social Services

    eWater

    Develops and supports a number of software tools for hydrological modelling and brings those tools to Australian and international markets. Some tools include Source, MUSIC, Rainfall Runoff Library, Stochastic Climate Library and SedNet.

    eWater Ltd.

    Exploring Democracy

    Through timelines, trails and people, discover the places, people and events that have shaped the Australian nation.

    Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

    Export Finance and Insurance Corporation

    Assists Australian exporters and investors by providing internationally competitive finance and insurance facilities to support their overseas contracts and investments.

    Export Finance and Insurance Corporation

    F-111 Fuel Tank Maintenance website

    Provides information for F-111 deseal/reseal workers and their families, and other F-111 fuel tank maintenance workers, with particular reference to the unique fuselage repair work undertaken and the health risks involved. 

    Department of Veterans' Affairs

    Fair Work Commission

    Australia’s national workplace relations tribunal. Responsible for maintaining a safety net of minimum wages and employment conditions, as well as a range of other workplace functions and regulation.

    Fair Work Commission

    Fair Work Ombudsman

    Provides information and advice about your workplace rights and obligations.

    More

    • Pay
    • Leave
    • Employee entitlements
    • Awards and agreements

    • Ending employment

    Fair Work Ombudsman

    Family Court of Australia

    Australia’s specialist court dealing with family law disputes. Hears appeals from decisions in family law matters of the Federal Circuit Court.

    Family Court of Australia

    Family Relationships Online

    Provides all families (whether together or separated) with access to information and services about family relationship issues.

    More

    • Family Relationship Centres
    • Family Relationship Advice Line1800 050 321.

    Department of Social Services

    Farm Diversity

    Allows you to search crop and animal production enterprise alternatives by type or location to discover which may be best suited to your farm.

    AgriFutures Australia
