A–Z of government sites

Menzies' 1941 Diary

Extracts from Menzies’ diary of his trip to London in 1941, a candid record of decision-making in foreign and military policy. Throughout the trip Menzies also filmed his experiences.

Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

Merino wool

Promotes merino wool and the use of wool for fashion and lifestyle.

Australian Wool Innovation Ltd

METeOR - Metadata Online Registry

METeOR is Australia’s repository for national metadata standards for health, housing and community services statistics and information.

Australian Institute of Health and Welfare

MICoR – Manual of Importing Country Requirements

A database of importing country requirements for the meat, fish, dairy, eggs, honey and processed foods, plants, and live animal export industries. It sets out the requirements that exporters and the Department of Agriculture must meet for products and commodities to be accepted for import into specific overseas countries.

Department of Agriculture and Water Resources

Migration Blog

Sharing the benefits and challenges on issues relating to the temporary entry and permanent migration programs in Australia.

Department of Home Affairs

Military Superannuation and Benefits Scheme

MilitarySuper is a partly funded, defined benefit superannuation scheme. It opened to new entrants of the Australian Defence Force (ADF) in 1991.

Commonwealth Superannuation Corporation

MindMatters

A mental health initiative for secondary schools that aims to improve the mental health and wellbeing of young people.

Beyond Blue Ltd

Minister's Council on Asylum Seekers and Detention

Provides independent advice to the Minister on policies, processes, services and programmes necessary to achieve the timely, fair and effective resolution of immigration status for people seeking migration outcomes in Australia.

Department of Home Affairs

Ministerial and Parliamentary Services

Provides information for parliamentarians and their electorate and personal staff. Includes entitlements handbooks.

Department of Finance

Ministry for the Arts

Provides programs and policies that encourage excellence in art, support for cultural heritage and public access to arts and culture.

Ministry for the Arts

Missing Persons

The National Missing Persons Coordination Centre supports the work of state and territory police in the investigation of missing persons. Works to reduce the incidence and impact of missing persons and to educate the Australian community about this issue.

Australian Federal Police

MoAD Oral Histories

The Museum’s oral history collection contains a wealth of personal recollection and insight into the building and its people during the era when it was home to the federal parliament. 

Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

MoneySmart

Free, simple guidance you can trust. Covers superannuation, banking, investing, borrowing and credit, insurance, budgeting, scams, retirement income planning and unclaimed money. Includes links to calculators and tools to help you with your personal finances.

Australian Securities and Investments Commission

Montevideo Maru – list of prisoners of war and civilian internees on board

The Montevideo Maru was carrying more than 1000 prisoners of the Japanese forces, most of them Australian, when it was sunk by an Allied submarine. Provides what is reliably thought to be the most complete list of those on board.

National Archives of Australia

Mrs Prime Minister – Public Image, Private Lives

Exhibition site reveals the ‘woman behind the title’, examining the unique contribution, style and structure each brought to the position of Mrs Prime Minister.

Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

Murray-Darling Basin Authority

Manages the water resources of the Murray-Darling Basin in a way that best meets the social, economic and environmental needs of the Basin and its communities.

Murray-Darling Basin Authority

Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

Helps people understand Australia's social and political history by interpreting the past and present and exploring the future, bringing alive the importance of Parliament in the lives of Australians.

Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

Museum of Australian Democracy: Collection – objects and rooms

The significance of Old Parliament House today lies in its historical and social value to the Australian people. Every part of this building tells a story. Explore the building, its objects and its stories.

Museum of Australian Democracy at Old Parliament House

My Aged Care

Helps you find up-to-date information about Australia's aged care system. It provides easier access to a full range of aged care services and hosts a number of features including aged care home details, help at home and carer information.

Department of Health

My Health Record

A secure online summary of your health information. You control what goes into it, and who can access it. You can choose to share your health information with your doctors, hospitals and other healthcare providers.

Australian Digital Health Agency

My Health Record Developer

Guides and resources for developers creating new software or integrating eHealth products into their software.

Australian Digital Health Agency

My School

Search the profiles of almost 10,000 Australian schools. Find information about schools of interest to you and compare their resources and performance with similar schools across the country.

Australian Curriculum, Assessment and Reporting Authority

My Skills

Provides a national directory of vocational education and training (VET) organisations and courses. It enables you to search for, and compare, VET courses and training providers. You can also explore courses by industry and skills in demand.

Department of Education and Training

mychild.gov.au

Find information on different types of child care and how to get assistance with the cost of child care. Also find links to other useful websites about children's health and wellbeing, parenting and family support services.

Department of Education and Training

Myfuture: Australia's career information service

An interactive, online career exploration service for 16 to 24 year old students wishing to explore their career options. Helps you see job possibilities, identify your own interests and skill areas, and set career goals and plans to achieve them.
