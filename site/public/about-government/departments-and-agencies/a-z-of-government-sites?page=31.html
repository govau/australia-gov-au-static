A–Z of government sites

Public Sector Superannuation Scheme

PSS is a defined benefit superannuation scheme. It opened to new members in 1990 and closed in 2005.

Commonwealth Superannuation Corporation

Published Notices (Insolvency Notices)

Publishes notices, including insolvency and external administration-related notices, including company deregistration.

Australian Securities and Investments Commission

Questacon – The National Science and Technology Centre

Promotes greater understanding and awareness of science and technology within the community, making that experience fun, interactive, and relevant.

Questacon - The National Science and Technology Centre

Quitnow – National Tobacco Campaign

Provides supporting information for the quitnow national tobacco campaign, providing smokers with motivation and support to help them stop smoking. 

Department of Health

R&D Tax Incentive Snapshot

Step-by-step guide designed to give small and medium sized companies an overview of the R&D Tax Incentive program.

Department of Industry, Innovation and Science

RAAF Aeronautical Information Service

Provides aeronautical information products for all ADF flight operations in support of Defence activities worldwide.

Department of Defence

RAAF Amberley Aviation Heritage Centre

The home of the F-111, based at RAAF Base Amberley. Find out about aircraft, aviation and Air Force collections on display, and visit the museum.

Department of Defence

Racism. It Stops With Me.

The National Anti-Racism Strategy campaign invites all Australians to reflect on what they can do to counter racism wherever it happens.

Australian Human Rights Commission

Racism. No Way.

Aims to tackle racism in schools in Australia, through providing teachers, school students, parents and governors with games, research and lesson ideas that explore the causes and effects of racism for practical use in the classroom.

Department of Education and Communities [NSW]

Rainfall Reliability Wizard

The Wizard is a software tool to assist in analysing Australia's long-term monthly rainfall record. It is not a predictive tool. It is intended to provide users with map-based indicators of rainfall means, variability and reliability.

Department of Agriculture and Water Resources

Re:think – Better tax, better Australia

The tax discussion paper begins a conversation on how we create a tax system that supports higher economic growth and living standards, improves our international competitiveness and adjusts to a changing economy and new opportunities.

REC Registry

Supports the Large-scale Renewable Energy Target (LRET) and Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES) by facilitating the creation, transfer and surrender of renewable energy certificates (RECs).

Clean Energy Regulator

ReCollections: a journal of museums and collections

An independent, peer-reviewed journal focusing on two main areas: museology and museum practice; and the history and interpretation of objects and the social and environmental history of material culture.

National Museum of Australia

Reform of the Federation White Paper

Provides issues papers and roundtable summaries. Work to improve federal financial relations and the transparency of government spending will now be progressed by the Council on Federal Financial Relations, and the Commonwealth, state and territory Treasuries. 

Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet

Regional Development Australia

A national network of 55 committees made up of local leaders who work with all levels of government, business and community groups to support the development of their regions.

Regional Development Australia Committees

Regional Telecommunications Review

A review to consider the adequacy of telecommunications services in regional, rural and remote parts of Australia was conducted by the Regional Telecommunications Independent Review Committee in 2015. Includes the submissions and the 2015 Report.

Department of Communications and the Arts

Registered Automotive Workshop Scheme

Allows for the importation and supply of used specialist or enthusiast vehicles to the market in Australia. Under this scheme only a RAW can import a used vehicle into Australia. Find a listing of approved workshops and their schedule of approved vehicle models.

Department of Infrastructure, Regional Development and Cities

Remembering 1914–18: Objects, Stories and Emotions

Explore life, love and war as experienced in a young nation a century ago. View our objects and their stories and contribute your own. 

National Museum of Australia

Remuneration Tribunal

Determines the pay of key Commonwealth offices including judges and related offices; senators and members of Parliament (MPs); chief executive officers (CEOs) such as the heads of departments; and other full-time and part-time public offices.

Remuneration Tribunal

Renewable Energy Target

Allows home owners to reduce the cost of their solar panels or solar water heater through the RET scheme.

Clean Energy Regulator

Repatriation Medical Authority

An independent statutory authority responsible to the Minister for Veterans' Affairs. Determines Statements of Principles (SOPs) for any disease, injury or death that could be related to military service, based on sound medical-scientific evidence.

Department of Veterans' Affairs

Reserve Bank of Australia

Conducts monetary policy, works to maintain a strong financial system and issues the nation's banknotes.

Reserve Bank of Australia

Reserve Bank of Australia Museum

Museum displays and exhibitions tell the story of Australia’s banknotes against the background of the nation’s economic and social development, from colonial settlement through to the current era.

Reserve Bank of Australia

Resources Sector Jobs Board

Allows people to apply for resource sector jobs, including those covered by Enterprise Migration Agreements (EMAs). The service provides practical assistance for job seekers and employers including workforce and recruitment advice.

Department of Jobs and Small Business

Respect.gov.au – Violence against women. Let's stop it at the start.

Provides supporting information for a campaign to prevent violence against women by teaching children to respect family and friends, and being mindful of gender stereotypes.

Department of Social Services
