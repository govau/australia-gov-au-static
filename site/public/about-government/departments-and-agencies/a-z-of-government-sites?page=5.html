A–Z of government sites

Australian Consulate-General, Shanghai, China

Australian Consulate-General in Shanghai, China.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Consumer Law

Provides an overview of the legislation and amendments, plus guides and fact sheets to explain the Australian Consumer Law in simple language. The law applies nationally, and to all Australian businesses.

Australian Criminal Intelligence Commission

Works with law enforcement partners to improve the ability to stop criminals exploiting emerging opportunities and perceived gaps in law enforcement information.  Creates a national intelligence picture of crime and targets serious and organised crime.

Australian Criminal Intelligence Commission

Australian Curriculum

The Curriculum sets out, through content descriptions and achievement standards, what students should be taught and achieve, as they progress through school. Includes the Foundation to Year 10 and the Senior Secondary curriculum.

Australian Curriculum, Assessment and Reporting Authority

Australian Curriculum, Assessment and Reporting Authority

Develops and administers national school curriculum and national assessments, collects and publishes school performance data, provides school curriculum resource services and provides support to teachers.

Australian Curriculum, Assessment and Reporting Authority

Australian Cyber Security Centre

Brings cyber security capabilities from across the Australian Government together into a single location. It is the hub for private and public sector collaboration and information sharing to combat cyber security threats.

Department of Defence

Australian Cybercrime Online Reporting Network

A national online system that allows people to securely report instances of cybercrime. Also provides advice to help people recognise and avoid common types of cybercrime.

Australian Criminal Intelligence Commission

Australian Defence Force Academy

A unique military institution which combines military education and training with academic study for an undergraduate degree. Develops the future leaders of the Australian armed forces through education and training that is academically and militarily challenging.

Department of Defence

Australian Defence Force Warfare Centre

Tri-service joint education and training unit.

Department of Defence

Australian Digital Health Agency

Provides information on digital health for health care providers and implementers. Responsible for the national digital health strategy including the design, development, delivery and national operations.

Australian Digital Health Agency

Australian Disaster Resilience Knowledge Hub

Provides research, resources and news relevant to disaster resilience and emergency management and includes statistics and information, photos, video and media about past disaster events.

Australian Institute for Disaster Resilience

Australian Early Development Census

A nationwide survey that shows how young Australian children have developed as they start full-time education. Collects data in areas such as physical health; social competence; and language and cognitive skills.

Department of Education and Training

Australian Egg Corporation Ltd

Provides marketing and research & development (R&D) services for the benefit of all stakeholders, principally egg producers. AECL is mainly funded through statutory levies.

Australian Egg Corporation Ltd

Australian Election Portal

Provides links to the electoral enrolment form and the electoral authorities of the Commonwealth, states and territories.

Electoral Council of Australia & New Zealand

Australian Electoral Commission

Responsible for conducting federal elections and referendums and maintaining the Commonwealth electoral roll. Also provides a range of electoral information and education programs and activities.

Australian Electoral Commission

Australian Embassy and Permanent Mission to the United Nations, Vienna

Australian Embassy and Permanent Mission to the United Nations in Vienna, accredited to Austria, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Hungary, Kosovo, Slovakia, Slovenia, and to the international organisations in Vienna.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Afghanistan

Australian Embassy in Kabul, accredited to Afghanistan.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Argentina

Australian Embassy in Buenos Aires, accredited to Argentina, Paraguay and Uruguay.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Brazil

Australian Embassy in Brasilia, accredited to Brazil.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Brussels

Australian Embassy in Brussels, accredited to Belgium, Luxembourg, the European Union, and NATO.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Cambodia

Australian Embassy in Phnom Penh, accredited to Cambodia.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Chile

Australian Embassy in Santiago, accredited to Chile, Colombia, Ecuador and Venezuela.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, China

Australian Embassy in Beijing, accredited to the People's Republic of China.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Croatia

Australian Embassy in Zagreb, accredited to the Republic of Croatia.

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade

Australian Embassy, Denmark

Australian Embassy in Copenhagen, accredited to Denmark, Norway and Iceland.
