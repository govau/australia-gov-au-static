What are apps?

Apps are pieces of computer software (applications) that allow you to customise your smartphone or tablet. Some apps (eg calendars and calculators) come preloaded on your smartphone and others (eg National public toilet map) can be downloaded from an app store (eg iTunes or Google Play).
