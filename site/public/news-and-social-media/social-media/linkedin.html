LinkedIn is a business-oriented social networking service, mainly used for professional networking.  Government company pages helps LinkedIn members learn about an agency's business and job opportunities.

ACARA is responsible for the development of a national curriculum, a national assessment program and a national data collection and reporting program that supports 21st century learning for all Australian students.

ACCC

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission. The ACCC promotes competition and fair trade in markets to benefit consumers, businesses, and the community.

Airservices Australia

Official Linkedin Company page for Airservices Australia. As Australia's air navigation service provider, it provides air traffic control, aviation rescue and fire fighting and air navigation services.

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australian Nuclear Science and Technology Organisation. ANSTO is one of Australia’s largest public research organisations and custodian of much of our country’s important science infrastructure, including the OPAL nuclear research reactor, the Australian Synchrotron, accelerators, cyclotrons and neutron beam instruments.

APRA

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australian Prudential Regulation Authority. APRA oversees banks, credit unions, building societies, general and life insurance and reinsurance companies, private health insurance, friendly societies, and most members of the superannuation industry.

Official Linkedin Company page for the Asbestos Safety and Eradication Agency. We provide a national focus on asbestos issues which go beyond workplace safety to encompass environmental and public health issues.

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australian Securities and Investments Commission. ASIC is Australia's corporate, markets and financial services regulator.

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australian Attorney-General's Department. The Department delivers programs and policies to maintain and improve Australia’s law and justice framework, and strengthen our national security and emergency management.

Australia Council for the Arts

Official Linkedin Company page for the Australia Council for the Arts. The Council promotes Australian arts both here and overseas. It invests in artistic excellence through support for all facets of the creative process and is committed to the arts being more accessible to all Australians.

Australia Post

Official Linkedin Company page for Australia Post. As connectivity transforms our lives, Australia Post is evolving to meet the future needs of businesses by providing trusted service solutions in etailing, retailing, communications, financial services and postal.

Australia Unlimited

Official Linkedin Company page for Australia Unlimited. Through Australia Unlimited the Australian Government is aiming to enrich Australia’s strong global image based on landscape and lifestyle, by promoting those aspects of Australian capability that are less well-recognised – innovation, technology, science and creativity.

Official Linkedin Showcase page for the Australia-China Council, strengthening foundations and developing new frontiers between Australia and China, Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan.

Australian Air Force Cadets

Australian Border Force
